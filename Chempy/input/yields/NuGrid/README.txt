#Naming scheme of yield tables and meaning 


*element*.txt tables contain elements.
*isotope*.txt tables contain isotopes.

*_delay*.txt tables contain yields based on the delayed explosion prescription.
*_rapid*.txt tables contain yields based on the delayed explosion prescription.

*_winds.txt tables contain yield from stellar winds only.
*_pre_exp.txt tables contain yields from winds and pre-explosive ejection.
*_total.txt tables contain the total ejected yields including wind and explosive contribution.
